About Me

Industrial engineer by degree, developer by desire and determination. Upon entering the workforce, a necessity to write code led to a love of code and a determination to become a developer. After self training and writing many software tools to aid productivity for my colleagues and my own work, I was eventually appointed to my current role as a software developer.

More About Me

From a child with overwhelming curiousity to both a high school and one time university dropout, I certainly didn't choose the easy path to arrive here today. I did, however, choose a path that taught all is possible with desire and determination.

I started my professional career with a foundation in Industrial Engineering, and shortly after added a M.S. Engineering Management. Despite an initial disdain for coding when confronted with C++, my coworkers assumptions that I knew programming and the desire to automate repetitive tasks brought gave me a second introduction. My relentless pursuit of continuous education in various software development technologies eventually propelled me over the degree requirement hurdles and into a position I love as a C# developer.
